About Angela

Since the age of nine, my passion for healthcare has led me to collaborative development of pre-med college courses and hands on experience with human cadavers. As I became involved in volunteer work in the hospital, biological study in college and work in the nursing field I have gained an understanding for what makes the modern hospital function.

My involvements have exposed me to the inefficiencies that are needed in such a multifaceted system. My goal is to eliminating these inefficiencies and to advance the technologies in healthcare to better the work environment for staff and the overall experience for patients.
